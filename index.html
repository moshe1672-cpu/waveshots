WaveShots Photographer Terms of Service — Version 1.0 (March 2026)

1. Your Relationship with WaveShots
You are an independent contractor, not an employee of WaveShots. You retain full responsibility for your own taxes, insurance, and equipment.

2. Eligibility
You must be at least 18 years old, provide accurate registration information, have legal right to sell your content, and comply with all applicable laws.

3. Fee Structure
Default split: WaveShots retains 20% of each sale; you keep 80%. WaveShots may negotiate custom fee arrangements, which will be communicated in writing via your dashboard.

4. Payouts
Payouts are processed via Stripe Connect. You must complete Stripe onboarding before receiving payments. WaveShots may withhold payment in cases of suspected fraud or buyer disputes.

5. Copyright
You retain full copyright of your photos. You grant WaveShots a non-exclusive license to display your work on the platform and in marketing materials.

6. Prohibited Content
You may not upload nudity, illegal content, exploitative imagery, or content that infringes third-party rights. WaveShots may remove any content at its discretion.

7. Taxes
You are solely responsible for your tax obligations. US photographers earning $600+ per year will receive a 1099-NEC.

8. Termination
WaveShots may terminate your account for violations of this Agreement. Unpaid earnings will be paid within 30 days of termination unless withheld due to fraud or dispute.

9. Limitation of Liability
WaveShots is not liable for indirect or consequential damages. Total liability is capped at fees paid to you in the prior 90 days.
